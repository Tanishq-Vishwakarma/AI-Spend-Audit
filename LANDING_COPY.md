## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no account, no login required. You see 
your full results before we ask for anything.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is pulled from official vendor pricing pages and 
verified at submission. AI tool pricing changes frequently — we 
note the verification date on every data point.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Your tool and spend data is saved only to generate your 
shareable report URL. We don't sell it or share it with third 
parties. Identifying details (email, company name) are never 
shown on the public report URL.

**Q: What if I'm already spending optimally?**
A: We'll tell you that honestly. No manufactured savings, no 
fake recommendations. "You're spending well" is a valid and 
useful audit result.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, 
Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies 
that overforecast or pivoted. If your audit shows significant 
savings potential, Credex may be able to help you capture even 
more of it through discounted credits.
